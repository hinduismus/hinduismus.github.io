What are the Vedas?

The Vedas are the most ancient and sacred texts in Hinduism. They are considered a direct revelation of divine knowledge.

Main Categories of Vedas:

  • Rigveda: Hymns praising the deities.
  • Yajurveda: Ritual instructions.
  • Samaveda: Melodies and chants.
  • Atharvaveda: Spells and incantations.
